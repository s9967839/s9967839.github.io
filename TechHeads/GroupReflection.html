I would describe my overall experience working in a group for this assignment as one of my best group works. Despite of joining the group little late, the members welcomed me without any hesitation. There was full participation and engagement from all the team members. The best part was that our meetings and conversations were enjoyable, relaxed, and at the same time, very fruitful. Besides, the team members’ contributions were tremendous.. It was easier to understand the assignment task clearly as we discussed the requirements together. Giving and receiving feedback from the team members on our work gave me confidence and motivation to complete the task.
Although we had hour-long meetings, I think that the only thing that could have improved was to have more sessions as they were one of the exciting parts of the group project. Overall, our group ‘ Tech heads’ performed well by showing flexibility, openness, honesty, enthusiasm, and dedication and I must say that this experience has improved my collaboration, communication and team work skill.
